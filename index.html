About the Project

Understanding Aquatic Ecosystems

The 'Living Labs' initiative focuses on using ponds and model aquatic organisms to understand complex biomechanisms related to the nutritional ecology of aquatic ecosystems, especially under the challenges posed by global warming and emerging threats.

Research Focus

This project employs various experimental units such as Zebrafish, Common Carp, and Eurasian Perch, focusing on different aspects of aquatic life to gather comprehensive data and insights.

Team and Collaboration

A collaboration among leading scientists and institutions, this project aims to provide groundbreaking insights into aquatic nutritional ecology, leveraging a multidisciplinary approach.
